Welcome. We are classmates at The Starter League and learning html and css ("front-end" languages for web development). With our instructors' help, we have learned so much. In fact, we are both excited to continue learning when the semester ends.

So, why a blog about bread? Glad you asked. In addition to our interest in front-end web development, we both love to eat; and that love extends to many foods. But, we agreed our "can't-live-without" favorite is bread. Also, the process of making bread is meditative, and the results are sustaining and a joy to share with others. We hope some of the recipes on our site inspire you, and we hope you find a bread to love and share.
